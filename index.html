About

Who are we?

In today's rapidly evolving world, choosing the right career path is crucial for aspiring engineers.
With countless options and ever-expanding technology landscapes, students often find themselves in need of guidance to navigate through the vast array of opportunities.
Recognizing this need, our group of five dedicated students from Pune Institute of Computer Technology, Dhankavdi, embarked on a mission to develop a comprehensive career counseling website for budding engineers.
During our academic journey, we witnessed numerous instances where fellow students struggled with career decisions. Many lacked proper guidance, which often resulted in confusion and apprehension. Inspired to make a difference, we conducted surveys and held discussions to assess the challenges faced by students when it came to career choices. The overwhelming response reinforced our belief that a well-designed career counseling website could significantly impact the lives of budding engineers.
